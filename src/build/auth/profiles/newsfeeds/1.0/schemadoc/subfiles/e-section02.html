The article section element represents a major structural division in an article .

The article section element is a specialization of the section element. It provides a content model to fit the unique requirements of article divisions.

Allowed parents

body and section

Allowed children

This element must not contain text.

This element may contain the following children: address, annotation, block, caption, citation, code, description, d:description, h, hd, list, object, p, quote, section and table
